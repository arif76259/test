About the Club

AIBA Business Club – Sylhet was established to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world business practice for students of the Army Institute of Business Administration (AIBA), Sylhet.

Mission: To develop competent, ethical, and confident future business leaders.

Vision: To be a leading university business club recognized for professionalism and impact.

Core Values: Leadership, Ethics, Professionalism, Teamwork, Innovation.

What We Do

Seminars & Workshops

Skill-oriented sessions led by academics and industry professionals.

Business Case Competitions

Analytical and strategic problem-solving through real-world cases.

Industrial Visits

Exposure to corporate operations and professional work environments.

Skill Development

Training in communication, leadership, and career readiness.
